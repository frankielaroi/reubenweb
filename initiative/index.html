Partner with Us: Transforming Africa Through Innovation and Industrial Development

At ReubeTrade Commodities Ltd, we are leading the charge to industrialize Ghana and serve Africa through transformative projects designed to create lasting economic, social, and environmental impact. These initiatives are in the planning phase, and we invite investment firms to join us in shaping Africa's future.

1. Gold Refinery: Making Ghana the Heart of Africa's Gold Processing

We plan to establish a state-of-the-art gold refinery in Ghana with the capacity to refine 1,000 tonnes of gold dore bars monthly. This facility will serve as a hub to purchase raw gold produced across Africa, process it in Ghana, and transform it into:

  • Gold bullion for international markets.
  • Gold coins for investors and collectors.
  • High-quality jewelry and other gold products for global export.

Why Invest?

  • Value Addition in Africa: By refining Africa's gold locally, we retain value within the continent and reduce dependency on foreign refineries.
  • Global Export Hub: Ghana will become a central processing and export hub for African gold products, meeting growing international demand.
  • Economic Growth: The project will drive job creation, enhance export revenue, and position Africa as a global leader in gold processing.

Your investment will support the creation of a secure, technologically advanced refinery that places Ghana at the forefront of the global gold market.

2. Fully Automated Rice Factory: Securing Africa's Food Supply

To reduce Ghana and Africa's reliance on rice imports, we plan to build a world-class rice processing factory in Ghana capable of processing 1,000 tonnes of rice per hour. The facility will utilize advanced automation to deliver high-quality rice to African markets.

Why Invest?

  • Reducing Import Dependency: Africa imports vast quantities of rice from countries like Thailand and Vietnam. Our factory will drastically reduce this reliance, retaining economic value within the continent.
  • Empowering Farmers: We will collaborate with local farmers by providing soft loans, training, and a guaranteed market, fostering productivity and economic growth.
  • Meeting Rising Demand: Africa's growing population presents an expanding market for staple foods like rice, offering immense opportunities for growth.

By partnering with us, your investment will transform Ghana into a major rice producer for the African market, improving food security and boosting local agriculture.

3. Renewable Energy: Building Africa's Green Energy Future

As part of our commitment to sustainability, we are investing in renewable energy projects to provide clean, affordable energy to Africa. Our flagship initiative involves constructing a solar power facility in Ghana with a capacity exceeding 100 Gigawatts monthly, aimed at serving industries, businesses, and communities across the continent.

Why Invest?

  • Abundant Solar Potential: Ghana's northern regions receive some of the highest levels of solar irradiation, making them ideal for large-scale solar energy production.
  • Meeting Africa's Energy Demand: Africa's energy needs are rising rapidly, and renewable solutions are essential to bridge the gap sustainably.
  • Climate Action: Renewable energy reduces greenhouse gas emissions and aligns with global efforts to combat climate change.
  • Cross-Border Opportunities: This facility will serve Ghana and neighboring countries, contributing to regional energy security and sustainability.

Your investment will help power Africa's green energy future, positioning your firm as a leader in sustainability while driving long-term returns.

Why Partner with ReubeTrade Commodities Ltd?

  • Strategically Based in Ghana: All our projects are based in Ghana, leveraging the country's resources, workforce, and strategic location, while serving consumers across Africa.
  • High-Growth Potential: Our initiatives target sectors with immense demand, including gold processing, agriculture, and renewable energy.
  • Impact-Driven Approach: These projects are designed not only to generate profits but also to drive industrialization, create jobs, and foster self-reliance across Africa.
  • Long-Term Vision: We are committed to building sustainable systems that ensure lasting benefits for generations to come.

Let's Build Africa's Future Together

We are seeking strategic investment partners to bring these transformative projects to life. Together, we can redefine Africa's future through industrialization, sustainability, and innovation.
