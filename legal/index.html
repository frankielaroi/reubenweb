Legal Policies

1. Commitment to Integrity

ReubeTrade Commodities Ltd. is firmly committed to upholding the integrity, transparency, and ethical standards of the global commodity trade sector. The commodity trade industry is vital to the global economy, and we are dedicated to ensuring all transactions comply with applicable laws and regulations.

2. Prohibition of Fraudulent Practices

ReubeTrade Commodities Ltd. condemns and strictly prohibits any form of fraudulent activity. Fraudulent practices, including but not limited to the submission of false or misleading documents, will be treated as serious violations of the law. The following documents are included under this prohibition:

  • Letters of Intent (LOI)
  • Irrevocable Corporate Purchase Orders (ICPO)
  • International Master Fee Protection Agreements (IMFPA)
  • Ready Willing and Able (RWA) letters
  • Bank Comfort Letters (BCL)
  • Soft Corporate Offers (SCO)
  • Full Corporate Offers (FCO)
  • Sales and Purchase Agreements (SPA)
  • Contracts

Submitting false documents or counterfeit products constitutes a criminal offense and will be reported to the appropriate authorities, including but not limited to:

  • Federal Bureau of Investigation (FBI), Washington, DC, USA
  • INTERPOL, IP Crime Unit, Lyon, France
  • International Criminal Court (ICC), Paris, France

3. Reporting Fraudulent Activity

If fraudulent activity is suspected or detected, ReubeTrade Commodities Ltd. reserves the right to report such actions to the aforementioned authorities. We take this responsibility seriously to protect the integrity of the global commodity trade and to deter criminal activities that threaten its stability.

4. Compliance with International Standards

ReubeTrade Commodities Ltd. complies with all international laws and regulatory frameworks governing the commodity trade sector. These policies are aligned with the measures agreed upon during the global meeting of regulatory bodies on November 15, 2008, to protect the industry.

5. Partner and Stakeholder Responsibility

We require all partners, clients, and stakeholders engaging with ReubeTrade Commodities Ltd. to adhere to these legal policies. By accessing or using our services, you agree to act responsibly, maintain transparency, and uphold the principles of honesty and integrity in all transactions.

6. Enforcement and Legal Consequences

Failure to comply with these legal policies may result in severe legal and financial consequences. ReubeTrade Commodities Ltd. reserves the right to terminate any agreements or partnerships found to be in violation of these policies. Legal action may also be pursued as deemed necessary.

7. Contact Information

For inquiries or to report suspected fraudulent activity, please contact the relevant authorities directly or notify ReubeTrade Commodities Ltd. at our official contact channels.

8. Updates to Legal Policies

ReubeTrade Commodities Ltd. reserves the right to update or amend these legal policies at any time to reflect changes in the law, regulatory requirements, or company practices. All updates will be published on our official website.

By engaging with ReubeTrade Commodities Ltd., all parties confirm their understanding of and agreement to these legal policies. Together, we can ensure the integrity, transparency, and success of the global commodity trade sector.
