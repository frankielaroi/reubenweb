Our Products

ReubeTrade Commodities Ltd offers a wide range of high-quality commodities, sourced from trusted suppliers around the world. We are committed to providing our clients with the best possible products and services, and we are always looking for new ways to improve our offerings.

Yellow Maize (GMO & Non-GMO)

ReubeTrade supplies high-quality yellow maize to meet the diverse needs of our global clients. Our yellow maize is carefully sourced from trusted producers in Africa, Brazil, India, ensuring it meets international standards for quality and safety.

Cashew Nuts

Our raw and processed cashew nuts are exported to markets in Asia, Europe, and the US, where they are highly sought after for snacks and culinary uses.

Soybean

We supply high-quality soybeans for food and industrial use and soybean meal, a protein-rich ingredient essential for poultry, livestock, and aquaculture feed.

Sugar (ICUMSA 45)

Known for its purity and quality, ICUMSA 45 sugar is ideal for food and beverage production. We source this premium sugar from trusted producers in Brazil.

Rice

We supply premium Grade A perfume rice, renownedfor its aromatic fragrance, superior quality, and exceptional taste from Thailand, Vietnam, and India.

Shea Butter

We supply premium 100% organic Shea butter, free from any addictive or mixtures. Our Shea butter is sourced from trusted partners in Ghana.

Gold

We source and supply high-quality gold bars and other gold products, ensuring compliance with international standards and regulations.

Crude Oil

ReubeTrade Commodities has established a global network of direct connections with oil producers and suppliers in key oil-producing countries, including: Saudi Arabia, United States, Russia, Netherlands, Nigeria, United Arab Emirates, Kuwait, Angola, Kazakhstan. Enabling us to source a wide range of petroleum products, including: Crude oil, Diesel EN590 10PPM and EN 50PPM, Liquified Natural Gas (LNG), Liquified Petroleum Gas (LPG), Gasoline (Petrol), Jet A-1 Fuel. We can allocate these products to buyers through various terminals, including: TTT/TTV (Rotterdam, Netherlands), Rotterdam (Netherlands), Fujairah(United Arab Emirates), Houston (USA), Novorossiysk (Russia), Aktau (Kazakhstan). Our direct connections with producers and suppliers ensure a reliable and efficient supply chain, allowing us to provide competitive pricing and flexible logistics solutions to our customers.

Industrial Goods

We offer a wide range of industrial goods, including energy equipment, construction equipment, and more. Our products are sourced from reliable suppliers and meet the highest quality standards.
