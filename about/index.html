About Us

ReubeTrade Commodities Ltd is a dynamic and forward-thinking company headquartered in Ghana, committed to sourcing and supplying high-quality commodities globally. With a strong focus on building strategic partnerships and delivering exceptional value to our clients, we specialize in a broad range of products, including agricultural goods, precious metals, industrial machinery, and energy sources. Our operations are driven by a commitment to excellence, sustainability, and ethical business practices.

Founded with the goal of bridging the gap between local producers and global markets, ReubeTrade Commodities partners with trusted suppliers and manufacturers from key regions around the world, including Africa, Asia, and the Americas. We take pride in ensuring that our products meet the highest standards of quality, purity, and reliability, enabling our clients to thrive in their respective industries.

At ReubeTrade Commodities, we understand the importance of adaptability and flexibility, offering tailored supply solutions to meet the unique needs of our diverse customer base, including wholesalers, retailers, manufacturers, and traders. Whether you're seeking agricultural commodities like rice, soybeans, or maize, precious metals such as gold and diamonds, or industrial machinery and energy solutions, we have the expertise and resources to deliver top-notch products on time, every time.

Our commitment to sustainability is reflected in our efforts to promote fair trade, responsible sourcing, and environmental stewardship throughout our supply chain. As part of our mission, we actively engage with local communities, contributing to their development and economic growth.

With a focus on quality, customer satisfaction, and a vision for long-term success, ReubeTrade Commodities Commodities Ltd is proud to be a trusted partner for businesses worldwide, driving economic growth and creating value in every transaction.

Mr. Awal Mohammed

Board Member

Mr. Awal Mohammed is a Board Member at ReubeTrade Commodities Ltd. He is an experienced accountant with over 11 years of expertise in commercial farming and commodity trading. Throughout his career, Mr. Mohammed has gained extensive knowledge in the agricultural sector and has been an active player in driving sustainable practices within the farming community.
