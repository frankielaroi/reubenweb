Organic Shea Butter

ReubeTrade Commodities is proud to supply 100% organic shea butter, sourced directly from skilled farmers in Ghana. Our shea butter is unrefined, free from additives, and retains all its natural nutrients and beneficial properties.

Sourcing & Production

We work closely with local farmers who harvest shea nuts from wild shea trees, following sustainable practices. The nuts are cleaned, roasted, and cold-pressed to extract pure, high-quality shea butter.

Key Benefits

  • 100% Organic: No chemicals or additives, ensuring purity and safety.
  • Nutrient-Rich: Packed with vitamins A, E, and F for skin and hair health.
  • Safe for Babies & Cooking: Gentle on sensitive skin and suitable for culinary use.
  • Sustainable & Fair Trade: Supporting local farmers with fair compensation and eco-friendly practices.

Applications

  • Skincare: Moisturizes, soothes, and heals dry skin and conditions like eczema.
  • Haircare: Nourishes dry or damaged hair, improving texture and shine.
  • Therapeutic Use: Ideal for massages and muscle relief.

ReubeTrade ensures rigorous quality control, delivering premium shea butter that meets global standards and supports sustainable, ethical sourcing.
